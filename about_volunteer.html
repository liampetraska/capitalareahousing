Advocate of the Year

Capital Area Housing Partnership extends their thanks to Tomie Raines Realtorsfor donating their services to CAHP in the selling of Avondale Square homes and in the listing of them in the Greater Lansing Association of Realtors’ MLS services at no cost. We know the imminent owners of affordable new homes in East Lansing will wish to offer their thanks as well!


Looking for ways to get involved with Capital Area Housing Partnership?

Listed below are a number of volunteer opportunities. If you are interested in assisting in one or more of these areas, please contact us at mdroste@hometownhousing.org or 517.332.4663. You can also fill out the online form below. If you have skills we’ve not thought of, remind us!

Volunteer opportunities available:

  • Social Media Management
  • Photographer
  • Fundraising Committee Members
  • Office Assistance
  • Distribution of Program Information
  • Proofreading
  • Advertisement Copywriting
  • Landscaping
  • Volunteer Coordination

Capital Area Housing Partnership also participates in numerous community events and needs friendly faces to staff our photo booth, hand out candy, and/or distribute program information at such events.

Head over to our Contact Us to email us to sign up for volunteer opportunities.

About CAHP

Capital Area Housing Partnership and Franklin Street, CHC, working in partnership with the community, promotes strong, diverse neighborhoods, affordable housing and homeownership and provides steps to greater financial independence in Mid-Michigan.
